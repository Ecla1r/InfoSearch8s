<p>The track is produced by <a data-api_path="/artists/27612" href="https://genius.com/artists/noah-40-shebib" rel="noopener">40</a>, <a data-api_path="/artists/133929" href="https://genius.com/artists/Trapmoneybenny" rel="noopener">TrapMoneyBenny</a> and <a data-api_path="/artists/149186" href="https://genius.com/artists/Blaqnmild" rel="noopener">BlaqNmilD</a>. <p>The track is heavily based on vocal samples, a staple of New Orleans bounce. One included sample is <a data-api_path="/artists/1811" href="https://genius.com/artists/Magnolia-shorty" rel="noopener">Magnolia Shorty</a>’s <a data-api_path="/songs/2075217" href="https://genius.com/Magnolia-shorty-smoking-gun-lyrics" rel="noopener">“Smoking Gun,”</a> and City Girls' vocals are written to sound similar to that track. Also sampled is <a data-api_path="/artists/4" href="https://genius.com/artists/Lil-Wayne" rel="noopener">Lil Wayne</a>’s <a data-api_path="/songs/1524" href="https://genius.com/Lil-wayne-lollipop-lyrics" rel="noopener">“Lollipop.”</a></p>
<p>At the end of the song, Drake samples the seventh episode of the second season of popular TV show <a href="http://www.fxnetworks.com/shows/atlanta" rel="noopener nofollow"><em>Atlanta</em></a>, which is written, directed, and acted in by Donald Glover a.k.a <a data-api_path="/artists/1745" href="https://genius.com/artists/Childish-gambino" rel="noopener">Childish Gambino</a>. This episode revolved around Drake and was aptly titled “Champagne Papi,” his <a href="https://www.instagram.com/champagnepapi/?hl=en" rel="noopener nofollow">Instagram</a> username and producer alter-ego.</p>
<p>Days following the album’s release, “In My Feelings” garnered widespread media attention due to a <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/BkoUbKJhFFz/?taken-by=theshiggyshow" rel="noopener nofollow">viral video</a> of comedian and Instagram personality, <a href="https://www.instagram.com/theshiggyshow" rel="noopener nofollow">Shiggy</a>, dancing to the song’s hook. The video evolved into the <a href="https://youtu.be/LTr8Gv_yx8Q" rel="noopener nofollow">#DoTheShiggy challenge</a> where people would try to replicate Shiggy’s original video. The challenge contributed to “In My Feelings” topping the Billboard Hot 100.</p>
