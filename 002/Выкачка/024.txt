class="RichText__Container-oz284w-0 gFPJcR"><p>Eminem and late rapper <a data-api_path="/artists/1237094" href="https://genius.com/artists/Juice-wrld" rel="noopener">Juice WRLD</a> team up for the first time on “Godzilla,” where they compare themselves to monsters. In particular, they become <a href="https://www.pastemagazine.com/blogs/lists/2014/05/kaiju-a-go-go-every-godzilla-monster-from-lamest-to-coolest.html" rel="noopener nofollow">Godzilla</a>, a fictional sea monster that is known for its mass destruction and endless killing.</p>
<p>While this track marks the first collaboration between the two artists, Juice previously <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gLWGQSAi3gM" rel="noopener nofollow">listed Em as one of his biggest influences</a> and has frequently referenced his music, most notably on his May 2018 track, <a data-api_path="/songs/3735927" href="https://genius.com/Juice-wrld-lean-wit-me-lyrics" rel="noopener">“Lean Wit Me.”</a> In October 2018, Juice joined <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC8MPOWNoWA-B4MS_qv9i3PQ" rel="noopener nofollow"><em>Tim Westwood TV</em></a>, where he <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fSoT13msPe4" rel="noopener nofollow">freestyled close to an hour over different Eminem beats</a>.</p>
<p>“Godzilla” also serves as Juice WRLD’s first posthumous release since his <a href="https://www.tmz.com/2019/12/08/juice-wrld-dead-dies-seizure-chicago-midway-airport-hospital/" rel="noopener nofollow">passing</a> on December 8, 2019—six days after his 21st birthday. Juice reportedly recorded the chorus for the track before his untimely death, although he was unable to complete his actual verse for the song in time. Later in January 2020, a <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/B7bSGjhl1Aj/?utm_source=ig_embed" rel="noopener nofollow">picture surfaced on XXL</a> that depicted Juice WRLD and his friends after he recorded the chorus.</p>
<p>Genius <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ssj0g83OfJE&amp;list=WL&amp;index=37&amp;t=0s" rel="noopener nofollow">broke down the song</a> on January 17, 2020, in an edition of their <em>Song Stories</em> series. In March 2020 <a data-api_path="/articles/10751" href="https://genius.com/a/interview-da-got-that-dope-on-producing-eminem-s-godzilla-juice-wrld" rel="noopener">interview with Genius</a>, producer d.a. got that dope broke down how the song came together.</p>
<p>The song would receive its first certification on February 5, 2020, when it was certified Gold by <a href="https://musiccanada.com/gold-platinum/?_gp_search=Godzilla%20Eminem" rel="noopener nofollow"><em>Music Canada</em></a> making it the first official certification for the single.</p>
<p>The official music video dropped on March 9, 2020, directed by <a href="https://www.imdb.com/name/nm9420496/?ref_=tt_ov_dr" rel="noopener nofollow">Cole Bennett</a>, one of the biggest names in music video production, and starred <a data-api_path="/artists/123" href="https://genius.com/artists/Dr-dre" rel="noopener">Dr. Dre</a>, retired boxer <a data-api_path="/artists/33976" href="https://genius.com/artists/Mike-tyson" rel="noopener">Mike Tyson</a>, Eminem, actress <a href="https://www.imdb.com/name/nm11041891/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cl_t1" rel="noopener nofollow">Anna Dahl</a>, and actor <a href="https://www.imdb.com/name/nm5158491/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cl_t4" rel="noopener nofollow">George Todd McLachlan</a>, portraying a young Eminem. Juice WRLD appears in the video as well using older stock footage.</p>
<p>Em launched the <a href="https://www.eminem.com/news/eminem-launches-godzillachallenge-triller" rel="noopener nofollow">#GodzillaChallenge</a> on February 25th, 2020, which <a href="https://twitter.com/Eminem/status/1232374897525108736" rel="noopener nofollow">encouraged fans to spit the fast third in creative ways</a>, lip syncing or rapping, on the <a href="https://www.triller.co/" rel="noopener nofollow">Triller</a> app in order to win a shout out and signed lithograph. He <a href="https://twitter.com/Eminem/status/1240376248817287171" rel="noopener nofollow">announced the winner on March 18th, Jack Sherlock</a>. The fan lip synced Em’s verse while simultaneously showing his collection of vinyl Eminem albums.</p>
<p>In March 2022, it was confirmed by Chris Long (Juice’s Videographer) that Juice WRLD had recorded two extra verses on the beat. There is currently no confirmation if they’ll ever be released.</p>
