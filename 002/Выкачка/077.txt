Camila <a href="http://www.popsugar.com/latina/Camila-Cabello-Her-Cuban-Background-42239921" rel="noopener nofollow">immigrated to the US</a> when she was still a kid:</p>

<p>The song was released as a “Summer Double Feature” along with <a data-api_path="/songs/3133056" href="https://genius.com/Camila-cabello-omg-lyrics" rel="noopener">“OMG”</a> from Camila Cabello’s debut album. As of now, it is her <a href="https://www.buzzfeed.com/eleanorbate/camila-cabello-havana-interview?utm_term=.gkv7P8KLQ#.wnZBNm4D7" rel="noopener nofollow">favorite song to perform</a>.</p>
<p>There are at least <a href="http://time.com/collection/american-voices-2017/4927699/american-voices-camila-cabello/" rel="noopener nofollow">nine versions</a> of the song, including a <a data-api_path="/songs/3225333" href="https://genius.com/Camila-cabello-havana-no-rap-version-lyrics" rel="noopener">solo version</a>, a <a data-api_path="/songs/3292598" href="https://genius.com/Camila-cabello-havana-spanglish-version-lyrics" rel="noopener">Spanglish version</a>, and a <a data-api_path="/songs/3279181" href="https://genius.com/Camila-cabello-and-daddy-yankee-havana-remix-lyrics" rel="noopener">Spanglish remix</a> featuring <a data-api_path="/artists/1823" href="https://genius.com/artists/Daddy-yankee" rel="noopener">Daddy Yankee</a>. <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZbLOwlemkac" rel="noopener nofollow">In an interview with 98.7 AMP</a>, Camila stated that Havana was the hardest song to write of the whole album:</p>
<blockquote><p>I have <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sXgvXrRul6E" rel="noopener nofollow">performed a version of it in Chicago</a> for a radio show that I had. <p>According to producer <a data-api_path="/artists/143754" href="https://genius.com/artists/Louis-bell" rel="noopener">Louis Bell</a>, “Havana” is definitely a “Camila song” and it <a href="http://www.mtv.com/news/3067275/camila-cabello-producers-interview-album/?xrs=_s.tw_news" rel="noopener nofollow">changed the tone</a> of her debut album:</p>
