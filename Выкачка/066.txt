Kardashian</a>. <p>Kanye first shared a short snippet of this song on his <a href="https://www.kanyewest.com/" rel="noopener nofollow">website</a> together with the album cover casing for <em>Yeezus</em>. Along with <a data-api_path="/songs/152640" href="https://genius.com/Kanye-west-new-slaves-lyrics" rel="noopener">“New Slaves”</a> and <a data-api_path="/songs/152933" href="https://genius.com/Kanye-west-black-skinhead-lyrics" rel="noopener">“Black Skinhead,”</a> “Bound 2” was one of three singles to be previewed before the album’s release. <blockquote><p>The whole song was written without that main sample; it was a last-minute thing. The song had kind of a lot of R&amp;B music in it. […] So we started by getting the sample to work in the song, and then taking out as much stuff as we could, and then in the chorus reducing what was this whole musical thing, just this sort of one ugly, distorted bass note. I was thinking like <a data-api_path="/artists/458124" href="https://genius.com/artists/Alan-vega" rel="noopener">Alan Vega</a> and <a data-api_path="/artists/155884" href="https://genius.com/artists/Suicide" rel="noopener">Suicide</a>, that kind of noise-synth minimal vibe.</p></blockquote>
